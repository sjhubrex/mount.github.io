Sarah Huibregtse, MS

I work as an Instructional Technology Coordinator for Mid-State Technical College in Wisconsin Rapids. At MSTC I assist faculty and staff with software and technology needs, so ultimately it is my opportunity to teach the teachers. At night I teach Technology courses for Insight Schools of Wisconsin online using Angel course management system and Elluminate.

Outside of school, I enjoy spending time with my family and friends. I enjoy running, biking (road & mountain), traveling, reading, working with computers, creating Web sites, digital photography, gardening, and playing with my cat (Tigger).
