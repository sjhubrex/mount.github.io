A Little About Me...

During the day I work as an Instructional Technology Coordinator for Mid-State Technical College in Wisconsin Rapids.  At MSTC I assist faculty and staff with software and technology needs.  I also teach evening and online Business and Information Technology courses for Mid-State Technical College and Madison College (MATC).

Cedar Grove, Wisconsin is my hometown.  Outside of school, I enjoy spending time with my family and friends.  I enjoy running, biking (road & mountain), traveling, reading, watching movies (Netflix), working with computers, creating web sites, digital photography, scrapbooking, gardening, and playing with my cat (Tigger).

Education:

  • Masters degree in Computer Science Education from Cardinal Stritch University
  • Bachelors degree from Lakeland College in Elementary Education with a minor in Computer Science
  • Web Site Development Certificate from Madison College (MATC)
  • Microsoft Office User Specialist Master Certification and Authorized Instructor (Office 2000)
