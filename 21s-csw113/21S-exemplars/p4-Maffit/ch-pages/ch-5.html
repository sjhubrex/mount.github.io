Ensuring Your Animation Run Really Smoothly

Chapter 5

When working with animations it is important that the transitions and the way it runs is smooth. What is a smooth animation? Well, from what I learned in this chapter a smooth animation is when they are responsive, run at 60 frams per second (fps), and are responsive and fast on mobile devices. Below I have worked with creating my very own transition that works smoothly in an animation. This was done by using what I have learned so far with animations and combining them in order to make a smooth version. I have have include a not so smooth version, so you can see the difference, I discuss why they are smooth and not smooth in my video description.
