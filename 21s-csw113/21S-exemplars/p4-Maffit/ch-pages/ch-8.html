Animating Your Links to Life

Chapter 8

In this chapter, I learned more about bringing in style to links on web pages I create. Links are everywhere and are in almost every webpage people will be creating or have created. By providing some sort of animated feedback when a user hovers over the links is utilizing animations in a very subtle and meaningful way.There are three different ways to animate my links that I learned in this chapter. I learned the following to animate links:

  1. Animated Underline which will underline the links when you hover over them. This underline won’t just suddenly appear, since in the style tag there is a text decoration showing only when you hover over the link.
  2. Simple Background Color Change which will changing the link’s background color when you hover over it.
  3. Cooler Background Color Change which is when instead of having the background color just fading into view, it actually slides into view.
