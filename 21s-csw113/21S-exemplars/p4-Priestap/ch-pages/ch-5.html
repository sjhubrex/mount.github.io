Chapter 5

Smooth transitions help with focus and engagement for web visitors. Nothing is worse then going on a website and spotting an animation with a rough transitions. This will loose the trust the visitor has in the webstie then resulting in a lost customer. That is why smooth tranistions come into play.


                

As seen above I have embedded two animatiosn one that is smooth and one that is not as smooth. I used the same code for each except I changed the transform of each of them to be different and also I changed the degree which they do the transition. Smooth transitions are responsive and they do not glitch when you hover over them or even click on them to activate. Making a smooth transition happen is playing around with different settings like the scale3d numbers and making sure they are at the appropriate levels for the smooth transition.

Overall Throughts

  • The positives is that every chapter led up to learning the smooth transitions. Which made learning how to do smooth tranistion much easier and time efficent.
  • The only downsides would be trying to think of how a web developer specifically wants their smooth tranistions to end up being displayed.
