Project 1 Submissions

Project 1 for this course is always a professor selected piece that all students develop during the first half of the semester. However, as you can hear how students enhance and personalize the piece is up to them and depends on what they decide to focus on. Project 1 the final version was submitted at the end of Week 6.

Please note, we do not claim to own the copyright to the music performed in these Python programmed pieces.
